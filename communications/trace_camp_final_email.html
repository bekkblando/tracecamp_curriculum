Important Trace Camp Information

One more week until Trace Camp officially starts on July 29th!

There are several things we would like to update you on:

1. Connect to our Slack workspace & introduce yourself

If you haven’t already, make sure you join our Slack workspace. That is our primary form of communication.

Once you connect, go to the `#welcome` channel and introduce yourself! Also be sure to add a profile picture to your account.

If you are looking for people to room or carpool with, we have created a channel called `#housing-n-rideshare` where you find other participants looking as well.

Connect to Slack

2. View updated pre-camp work

We have updated the pre-camp work with additional software to install. Make sure that if you have Windows you install the Windows Subsystem for Linux (WSL) and install all the software on that.

View Pre-Class Work

3. Attend our install day for help

If you need help installing any of the software, help with any of the pre-camp work, or just want to meet us before the camp, we will be hosting an Install Day on 28th from 1-6pm in McAdams Hall. The door will be locked so when you get there message one of the instructors on Slack or leave a message in the `#general` channel.

4. Let us know about dietary restrictions

If you have any dietary restrictions or suggestions for food please fill out our Trace Camp Dietary Restrictions & Suggestions Survey.

5. Follow our Calendar

All Trace Camp events will be added to this calendar. As a reminder, Trace Camp will take place from 07/29/19 – 08/16/19, Monday – Saturday.

On Mon – Fri we will be in the Watt Family Innovation Center and Saturdays will be in McAdams Hall. Additionally, on 08/16 you will be demonstrating your final projects in Greenville at the Atlas Local coworking space in Greenville.
